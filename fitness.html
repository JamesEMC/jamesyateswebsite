Fitness Journey

Progress, recorded properly.

This page is a small public snippet of my Spartan training progress following my June 2025 result. My goal is to better that result next year, place higher, and make the work visible through simple, honest measures that can be tracked over time.

The focus is not just one race-day target. It is the steady improvement between June 2025 and the next Spartan event: increased leg strength for climbs and carries, better running speed, more upper-body pulling strength, and better conditioning when the course gets uncomfortable.

Public view

Visitors can view the charts only. Return home. In a live version, the training data would be updated through a private admin page or dashboard, so the public website remains clean while the progress records stay easy to maintain.

EMC Engineering

Practical EMC tools from real test work.

My EMC experience has been built over more than 20 years across commercial, aerospace, defence and space test environments. It has included hands-on conducted and radiated emissions, susceptibility testing, power input work, antenna use, receiver setup, standards interpretation, uncertainty, lab management and practical fault-finding under programme pressure.

That background has taken me through commercial EMC test work, Airbus spacecraft and payload environments, Element and EVT lab leadership, Mangata spacecraft test planning and my current Lead EMC Engineer role at Leonardo. The pages below are tools I have created along that journey to make common EMC calculations, setup decisions and teaching points easier to understand and repeat.

Where it comes from

From tracking radars to near-field ranges.

My first antennas were part of the radar systems at QinetiQ Aberporth, tracking aircraft entering a weapons test range and pulling telemetry from targets and payloads. At Powerlink the scale shrank to the bench: RF amplifiers, tuning, and test equipment you could hold.

Airbus brought my first planar range and pulled antenna measurement, EMC and spacecraft qualification together. At Mangata, as Director of Spacecraft Test, that turned into working with suppliers on how a spherical near-field range could be built into a satellite test facility in Scotland — antenna measurement not as a test, but as a building.

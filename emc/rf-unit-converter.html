From dBµV

Let Z = impedance in ohms and U = µV.

  • dBmV = dBµV - 60
  • dBV = dBµV - 120
  • dBµA = dBµV - 20 log10(Z)
  • dBmA = dBµV - 20 log10(Z) - 60
  • dBA = dBµV - 20 log10(Z) - 120
  • dBpW = dBµV - 10 log10(Z)
  • dBm = dBµV - 10 log10(Z) - 90
  • dBW = dBµV - 10 log10(Z) - 120
  • µV = 10^(dBµV / 20)
  • mV = 10^((dBµV - 60) / 20)
  • V = 10^((dBµV - 120) / 20)
  • µA = 10^((dBµV - 20 log10(Z)) / 20)
  • mA = 10^((dBµV - 20 log10(Z) - 60) / 20)
  • A = 10^((dBµV - 20 log10(Z) - 120) / 20)
  • pW = U² / Z
  • mW = (U² / Z) × 10^-9
  • W = (U² / Z) × 10^-12

To dBµV

  • dBµV = dBmV + 60
  • dBµV = dBV + 120
  • dBµV = dBµA + 20 log10(Z)
  • dBµV = dBmA + 20 log10(Z) + 60
  • dBµV = dBA + 20 log10(Z) + 120
  • dBµV = dBpW + 10 log10(Z)
  • dBµV = dBm + 10 log10(Z) + 90
  • dBµV = dBW + 10 log10(Z) + 120
  • dBµV = 20 log10(µV)
  • dBµV = 20 log10(mV) + 60
  • dBµV = 20 log10(V) + 120
  • dBµV = 20 log10(µA) + 20 log10(Z)
  • dBµV = 20 log10(mA) + 20 log10(Z) + 60
  • dBµV = 20 log10(A) + 20 log10(Z) + 120
  • dBµV = 10 log10(pW) + 10 log10(Z)
  • dBµV = 10 log10(mW) + 10 log10(Z) + 90
  • dBµV = 10 log10(W) + 10 log10(Z) + 120

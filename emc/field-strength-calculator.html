Inputs

Results

Far-field, free-space maths — no chamber reflections, no near-field gain reduction, no cable loss. For a test level like RS103's 20 V/m, add real margin for modulation (a 1 kHz 80% AM CW signal needs the amplifier to deliver the peak, not the RMS) and check the antenna's spec at your actual distance.

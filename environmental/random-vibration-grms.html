Random vibration gRMS

A random vibration spec is a set of PSD breakpoints — frequency against g²/Hz. The overall severity is the square root of the area under that curve, integrated properly on log–log axes where the segments are straight lines, not the flat trapezoids a spreadsheet would give you.
